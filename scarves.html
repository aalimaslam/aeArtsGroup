Scarves

Pashmina or "soft gold" is a Kashmiri woven wool textile from four distinct breeds of Cashmere goats. These are hand-made scarves, stoles, and shawls can be of pure Cashmere wool that is lightweight and delicate to the touch, and can also be a mix of silk and Cashmere wool that is tightly woven, with an elegant sheen, and still delicate to the touch.
